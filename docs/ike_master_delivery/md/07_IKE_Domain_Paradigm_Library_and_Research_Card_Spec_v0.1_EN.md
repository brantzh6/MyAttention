# IKE Domain Paradigm Library and Research Card Spec v0.1

## 1. Paradigms Matter

Different domains require different research paradigms. A field should not be represented only by entities and facts, but also by competing ways of explaining and studying the field.

## 2. Core Objects

Use Paradigm and ResearchCard as first-class objects. Paradigm defines assumptions, preferred evidence, typical questions, and common failure modes. ResearchCard binds a concrete study to one or more paradigms.

## 3. Starter Packs

Initial paradigm packs should be prepared for AI, economics, life sciences, and other priority domains.

## 4. Evolution

Paradigms can be admitted, split, merged, downgraded, frozen, or retired. The library is not static.
