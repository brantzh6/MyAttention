# IKE Research Workflows and Story Templates Spec v0.1

## 1. Workflow Families

Five families are recommended: field foundation research, entity-centered research, problem-driven judgment, change tracking, and self-upgrade research.

## 2. Canonical Seven-Step Workflow

Trigger → scope → gather evidence → structure and compare → select thinking model(s) → produce output and uncertainty → review and next action.

## 3. Story Templates

Template families should be reusable across domains but parameterized by field and paradigm.

## 4. Integration

Every workflow must bind to object contracts, model governance, and harness evaluation.
